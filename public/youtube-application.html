My Education Expedition

All through my early schooling as a kid I enjoyed learning. As the years carried on it became a humdrum of the same things. That was boring and I quit liking school. I then went on to college because what else was I going to do. I didn't really enjoy having to sit through hour long lectures with nothing to do so I wound up skipping a lot of classes and teaching myself. I enjoying learning all the new topics, but really didn't like the lecture style of teaching.

Tutorial Hell

After college I got a job at a local hospital as an IT Tech. I've been there just over 8 years now and about to become an IT Security Analyst. I spent this time learning meaning things which you can read about here. I always enjoyed coding and creating things which lead to a lot of tutorials and winding up in tutorial hell. It wasn't until I found ZTM that I figured out a plan to become a programmer.

Enter ZTM Academy

I wanted to learn web developement and found the ZTM course through reddit. I got it from Udemy and started going through it then life got crazy and it got put on the back burner. When I wanted to try again, I found the ZTM Academy and got the lifetime membership so I could learn everything. I've completed 4 courses and a few workshops so far with plans to do more. I was able to complete a few good projects and add my features to them including my personal site, Random Flea. I'm really enjoying the Sprint right now. It's a great pace to not get overwhelmed and really learn the material.
